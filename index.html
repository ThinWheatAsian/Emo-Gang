The Berserker is a Common card that is unlocked from the Jungle Arena (Arena 9) It spawns a fast, single-target, ground-targeting, melee, ground troop with moderate hitpoints and low damage. She appears as a kid who wears a light brown dress made of bear skin, dark brown wristbands, and a team-colored belt. She has orange hair, eyes and eyebrows, a loose tooth, and a scar on her left chin, arms and legs, and wields a pair of cleavers. A Berserker card costs 2 Elixir to deploy.
